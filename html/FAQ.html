Frequently Asked Questions



  • Question 1: What is the use of this website?
    • MyMovieLocker keep track of watched movies, rate and review them. Create lists, choose from a catalog or request to add a movie to the database. Chat with similar movie fans or debate with opposing reviewers.

  • Question 2: What if the movie you are searching for is not present in the database?
    • In the contact page, you can send a concern about not having a movie not in the database, and we can add it ASAP.

  • Question 3: Can I seperate my reviews of movies into groups?
    • Yes! We have a main feature of creating LOCKERS, which are like playlists for movies and adding as much movies you would like into as many lockers you have created.
